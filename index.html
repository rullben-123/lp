Profile

Hi! I'm Ruben Gutierrez and I am website designer in Cypress, California who enjoys helping people solve their problems. I've been building and designing websites for 4 years and I enjoy helping others and what I do.

When I’m not working on websites, I contribute to open source design by releasing free templates which other designers use as reference to build their websites. I do this because I believe helping others makes the world a better place especially in times like these where everyone is facing uncertainty. My templates have been downloaded thousands of times by individuals and companies all around the world. Don’t be afraid to say hi, I always enjoy a good conversation!

Expertise

My Focus Is To Help You Keep Focus

Here are some ways I can help you improve your website

  • Website Design

    Whether you need a new website or you need to redesign your exisiting WordPress theme, I'm ready to listen and make your website look beautiful and unique to you and your business.

  • Small Business Tools

    Your website should be more than looks, It should also help you run your business and communicate with current/potential patrons a lot easier.

    Below are some of the tools and features I can add to your website to make your life a lot easier.

    - Contact Forms, receive a notification when someone wants to inquire about your products/service
    - Forms, have your customers fill out forms on your website without the hassle of a pen and paper
    - Portfolio/Image Galleries, exhibit your past work and testimonials
    - Pricing Menus, display the prices of your products/services in a way that makes it easier for Google to find them
    - Visitor Tracking, keep track of who your customers are and where they come from
    - Visitor Heatmap Tracking, see where you customers are focusing on each page
    - Databases, organize customer interactions/profiles/email addresses/ etc...
    - Automated Appointment Scheduler, give customers the ability to schedule appointment based on dates and times that work for you
    - Automated Email Responding, make sure customers don't feel unattended to by replying to messages as soon as you receive them
    - User Login/Membership Access, give access to premium content you may offer
    - A Blog, keep your customers updated about the latest information regarding your business
    - Adding a Comment Sections to your pages to increase user engagement
    - An Online Store
    - Weekly Email Newsletter, reach customers directly & immediately through mass email and inform them about promotion/sales/etc...

    - Social Media Integration
